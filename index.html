A guide explaining everything about how the game works

First steps

After pressing the "START" button we start on the "character selection" screen. In this screen we need to choose 4 characters from the 8 available. To choose any of the characters we must click on its file. If we click on the file of a character that was already chosen, we will uncheck it.

When we have chosen the 4 characters we want to play with, the "GO TO THE BATTLE" button will appear, which will lead us to the game.

We started the game

When loading the game, our 4 chosen characters (aka heroes), our enemy (aka dragon) and the "START YOUR TURN" button appears. By pressing the button, will charge the life and mana bars of our heroes, the life bars and dragon mana and the abilities of our 4 heroes.

The character images are arranged from top to bottom, and the health and mana bars and the lists of skills are ordered from left to right.

Playing the game

To play we can interact with 2 things: the skill lists of our characters and the button "END YOUR TURN".

Ability lists: we can choose an ability of each character to execute during the turn (throw a total of 4 skills per turn). We can see how much mana the skill spends next to its name, and a brief description of what it does by hovering over it. When we select a skill from a list, said list will be disabled and you will not be able to choose another skill or deselect the marked skill.

"END YOUR TURN" button: when pressing the button, the skills that we have marked will be saved to be executed (It is not necessary to have 4 skills marked to be able to press the button, only the ones that we have marked will be executed). Now there will be a series of animations in which the heroes attack, the dragon attacks, and the health and mana of all those affected by the combat will show the changes they have.

End of game

The objective is very simple: BEAT THE DRAGON. Look for the strongest characters, the best combinations and the hidden tricks to be able to finish with your enemy.

Characters

Here you can see a list with all the characters: a description of them, their stats and their abilities.

The "Attack" stat determines how much damage each ability will do (with a few exceptions).
